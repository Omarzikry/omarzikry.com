WHAT WE DO

Leadership in Life science investing

Anvil Holdings is a world-leading life science investor with a focus on creating long-term value.

Our mission is to enable and to make a positive and meaningful impact on health, science and society by generating attractive, long-term investment returns on our holdings and investments.

ABOUT US

Holdings and investments

We are the holding company of the Novo Group and manage the investment assets of the Novo Nordisk Foundation. We invest in life science companies at all stages of development and in a broad portfolio of equity and fixed income securities.
